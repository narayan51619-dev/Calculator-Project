About Calculator

What is a Calculator?
A calculator is an electronic device or software application used to perform mathematical calculations quickly and accurately.
It helps users solve arithmetic operations such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.


Where are Calculators Used?
Calculators are used in many places, including:

  • Schools and colleges for solving mathematics problems.
  • Banks and financial institutions for calculations related to money and accounts.
  • Businesses for accounting and budgeting.
  • Engineering and scientific fields for complex calculations.
  • Daily life for tasks such as shopping, bill calculations, and budgeting.
